Why Italy?

Italy Travel Guide

Italy is one of Europe's most iconic and popular destinations. Home to incredible food, fabulous wine, tons of ancient ruins, and picturesque landscapes, it should come as no surprise that this is one of the most sought-after travel destinations in the world.

Italy is best experienced slowly so pace yourself. Soak in the atmosphere and way of life as you explore. Relax, take in the scenery, enjoy a cappuccino or a glass of wine. The slower you go, the better you'll be able to appreciate the charms and nuance of this iconic southern European gem.

Favorite cafés in Italy

Licchio's

At Licchio's, guests may order delicious craft beer, cordial or Spritz. Take your chance to taste great cappuccino, lemonade or espresso. This place is famous for its great service and friendly staff.

Address:

Largo Santa Caterina, 7, 98039 Taormina ME, Italy

What I love about it

Great place for breakfast in Taormina with the view to a cozy Umberto square. It's prime for people watching if that's something you enjoy!

Manari

This café offers its visitors Italian cuisine. Do not go away without eating good gelato, and brioches. A lot of visitors order great cappuccino or delicious freshly-squeezed juice.

Address:

Via Matermania 2, Capri, Italy

What I love about it

A small place where you can enjoy delicious pastries and kind service. Plus espresso at a reasonable price.

Moon

Spend a nice time here and share perfectly cooked gelato, che and yogurt with your friends. Delicious cordial or good wine are among the best drinks to order.

Address:

Via della Marina, 4, 19025 Portovenere SP, Italy

What I love about it

Nice spot for a quick bite and a drink. Great outdoor space with views of the marina!
